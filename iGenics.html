The Modern Eye Challenge

Before delving into the details of iGenics, it's crucial to understand the challenges that modern lifestyles pose to our eyes. Our eyes are not designed to handle the prolonged exposure to screens and artificial lighting that has become so ubiquitous in our lives. This overexposure can lead to digital eye strain, dry eyes, and even more severe conditions like myopia (nearsightedness) and age-related macular degeneration.

Digital Eye Strain

Digital eye strain, often called computer vision syndrome, is a common ailment among those who spend long hours in front of screens. Symptoms include eye fatigue, dryness, blurred vision, and headaches. The blue light emitted by screens is a major contributor to digital eye strain.

Myopia

Myopia, or nearsightedness, is a condition where distant objects appear blurry. It's on the rise, especially among younger generations who spend more time indoors and on screens. Myopia can lead to more severe eye conditions if left untreated.

Age-Related Macular Degeneration (AMD)

AMD is a leading cause of vision loss among older adults. It damages the macula, a small part of the retina responsible for central vision. Prolonged exposure to UV light and oxidative stress are factors that increase the risk of AMD.
