Vision is a fundamental sense that shapes our interactions, learning, and overall quality of life. The eyes' complex structure, including the cornea, lens, and retina, work harmoniously to create visual perceptions. However, various factors, such as genetics, age, digital device usage, and environmental stressors, can challenge the integrity of our vision.

Key Ingredients and Their Benefits

a. Lutein and Zeaxanthin: These powerful antioxidants are naturally present in the retina and are known to protect against harmful high-energy light waves like ultraviolet rays. Lutein and Zeaxanthin may reduce the risk of age-related macular degeneration (AMD) and cataracts while supporting overall retinal health.

b. Bilberry Extract: Rich in anthocyanins, Bilberry Extract has been associated with improved night vision and reduced eye fatigue, making it a valuable addition to iGenics' formula.

c. Beta-Carotene: A precursor to Vitamin A, Beta-Carotene is crucial for maintaining good vision. It contributes to the health of the retina and supports the eye's ability to adjust to different lighting conditions.

d. Zinc: An essential mineral, Zinc is vital for transporting Vitamin A from the liver to the retina, playing a pivotal role in maintaining clear vision.

e. Vitamin C and E: These potent antioxidants combat oxidative stress and protect the eyes' delicate tissues from damage caused by free radicals.
