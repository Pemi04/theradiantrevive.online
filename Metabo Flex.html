Understanding Joint Health and the Challenges

Before delving into Metabo Flex, it's crucial to understand the basics of joint health and the challenges people face. Joints are the connections between bones that allow movement and flexibility. However, these complex structures are susceptible to wear and tear, inflammation, and degeneration as we age.

A. Common Causes of Joint Discomfort

Osteoarthritis: The most prevalent form of arthritis, osteoarthritis occurs when the protective cartilage that cushions the ends of bones wears down over time.

Rheumatoid Arthritis: An autoimmune disease where the body's immune system attacks the joints, leading to inflammation and joint damage.

Joint Injuries: Sports injuries, accidents, and trauma can cause joint damage, leading to pain and limited range of motion.

Age-Related Changes: As people age, joint tissues may become less elastic and more prone to damage, causing discomfort.
