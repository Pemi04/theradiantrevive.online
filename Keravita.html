Understanding Fungal Infections and Foot Health

Fungal infections, commonly known as fungal nail or athlete's foot, are prevalent foot conditions caused by microscopic fungi. These infections can thrive in warm, moist environments, such as sweaty shoes and damp socks, leading to symptoms like discolored, brittle nails, itching, and discomfort. Proper foot hygiene, breathable footwear, and early treatment play a crucial role in preventing and managing fungal infections.
